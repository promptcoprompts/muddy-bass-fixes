The Quick Fix

Short answer: In muddy water, throw dark high-contrast baits with vibration and noise (black & blue jigs, Colorado-blade spinnerbaits, rattling squarebills). For line twist/tangles: close the bail by hand every cast, spool with tension in the same direction as the factory spool, and soak mono/fluoro in warm water to kill memory.
