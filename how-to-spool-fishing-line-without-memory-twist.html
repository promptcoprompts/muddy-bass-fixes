How to Spool Fishing Line Without Memory Twist

Direct answer: Spool in the same direction the line comes off the factory spool, apply steady tension, and soak the new line in warm water afterward to kill memory.

Correct spooling steps

  • Tie the new line to the spool arbor with an arbor knot
  • Hold the line spool so it unwinds in the same direction your reel turns
  • Apply tension (use a pencil through the spool or have someone hold it)
  • Fill the spool to about 1/8 inch below the rim
  • Soak the filled spool in warm water for 15 minutes to reset memory
